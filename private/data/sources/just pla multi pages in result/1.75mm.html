All our 1.75mm PLA filament is made from pure virgin PLA with an extended range of beautiful semi-translucent and opaque Colors.  All our material is compatible with all 3D printers that use 1.75mm filament and has been extensively tested on a variety of printers.   We're convinced you'll find something you like.

Our lineup includes:

  1. Chroma-Line»1.75mm»PLA: Our line of Naturworks 4043D PLA, with an extended range of vibrant solid and translucent colors.
  2. Studio-Line»1.75mm»PLA: Our high quality Naturworks 4032D PLA, less brittle with more subtle realistic colors, excellent diameter consistency for precision prints.
  3. Pro-Line»1.75mm»PLA:  Made in the US,  high quality pure Naturworks PLA with medical grade color pellets, laser based quality control guarantees an excellent diameter consistency.
