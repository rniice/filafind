SemiFlex™ Flexible Filament

SEMIFLEX™

THE INDUSTRY LEADING FLEXIBLE FILAMENT

Just like the original NinjaFlex 3D filament, SemiFlex material boasts flexibility, strength and reliability for our 3D printing projects, and is slightly more rigid to expand your printing possibilities.

All SemiFlex filaments are made in the USA and are available in 1.75mm (500 grams) and 3mm (750 grams).

SHOP NOW  »

KEY FEATURES:

  • Consistent diameter and material properties providing reliable, high quality prints
  • Excellent impact resistance
  • Excellent abrasion resistance
  • Excellent chemical resistance
  • Excellent build platform adhesion and bonding between layers
  • Excellent post-processing capabilities
  • REACH and RoHS 2002/95/EC Directive compliant
  • Shore Hardness = 98A

PRINTING GUIDELINES:

  • Print Temperature Target:   230 (Range 225-235)
  • Print Bed Temperature: Ambient to 40C.
  • Print Bed Preparation: No special preparation is required.
  • Print Speed:
    • Top and bottom layers: 10-20mm/s
    • Infill speeds: 15mm/s – 35mm/s
  • Cooling Fans: ON if available

APPLICATIONS:

  • Best used with prints requiring a high level of detail, high resolution text or intricate parts
  • Great for use on unsupported vertical printing
  • Excellent in shock-absorption applications
