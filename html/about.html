About

FilaFind was created to aggregate materials for consumer 3D printers to help makers discover the materials needed for their next project.

DISCLAIMER: Properties are automatically generated from data provided from manufacturer or supplier website. These properties are subject to change without notice. Always check with the supplier or source for the latest available information on the product you are seeking to use.

CONTACT: If you are a supplier and would like to have your materials added to the database, please reach out to us via email at contact@markedmethod.com and I will share instructions for verifying and adding you as a supplier.

CREDIT: Materials Database is made possible using components of the Spark 3D Printing Platform. Spark is an open platform for building better 3D printing software, hardware, materials and services.
